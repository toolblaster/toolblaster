AMFI & SEBI Regulatory Cautionary Statement:

Public Provident Fund (PPF) is a statutory sovereign tax-saving instrument framed under the Government of India PPF Scheme, 2019. It offers capital protection and fixed interest income quarterly announced by the Ministry of Finance. PPF is not a market-linked mutual fund scheme and does not carry market volatility risks.

Calculations displayed on this platform are for educational projection purposes only and do not constitute direct AMFI/SEBI financial advise or portfolio solicitation. Investors are advised to align investments with individual risk horizons under Section 80C.
