SSLs.com Certificate Types Explained

SSLs.com offers a variety of certificates to match your specific needs. Choosing the right one can save you money and headaches.

4.1 Domain Validation (DV SSL)

Best For: Blogs, portfolios, and small business sites.

The most popular choice. It verifies domain ownership via email or DNS. Issued in minutes. No paperwork required.

4.2 Organization Validation (OV SSL)

Best For: Corporate websites and registered businesses.

Verifies that your business is a legal entity. Provides higher trust than DV but takes 1-3 days for vetting.

4.3 Extended Validation (EV SSL)

Best For: E-commerce, banks, and fintech.

The highest level of trust. Activates the green address bar (in some browsers) and displays your company name in certificate details.

4.4 Wildcard SSL

Best For: Growing sites with subdomains.

Secures your main domain (e.g., mysite.com) AND unlimited subdomains (blog.mysite.com, shop.mysite.com) with one cert.

Yes. When you buy an SSL, you get the certificate files. You can install these files on any hosting provider (Bluehost, HostGator, AWS, DigitalOcean, etc.) that allows custom SSL installation.

6 Does SSLs.com offer Wildcard SSLs?

Yes, they offer affordable Wildcard SSLs (starting around $35/year) that secure your main domain and unlimited subdomains (e.g., blog.yoursite.com, shop.yoursite.com).

7 How fast is the issuance?

For standard Domain Validated (DV) certificates, issuance is typically instant or within minutes after you confirm domain ownership via email or DNS record. OV and EV certificates take longer (1-3 days) due to business vetting.
