Privacy Policy & Terms

At Toolblaster, we believe that your daily focus and your personal data belong entirely to you. We've built DECIDE with a privacy-first, local-first architecture.

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies specifically to the DECIDE tool provided by Toolblaster. Our core promise is simple: we do not want your data, and we do not collect it.

Local-First Storage

DECIDE operates entirely inside your web browser. All of your entered priorities, completed tasks, streaks, and settings are saved locally on your device using browser `localStorage`.

None of this data is ever transmitted to our servers. We have no databases, no user accounts, and no way to see what you are typing into the app. Because of this, if you clear your browser data or switch to a different device, your history will not carry over unless you manually use the Export/Restore feature.

Analytics & Tracking

While the DECIDE tool itself does not track your personal inputs, the broader Toolblaster website may use standard, anonymous analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand general traffic patterns (e.g., how many people visit the page daily). This tracking is purely aggregated and contains no personally identifiable information (PII) linked to your daily tasks.
