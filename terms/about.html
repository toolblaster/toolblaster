Building Better Utilities.

High-performance tools for business, finance, and life.

Toolblaster was born from a simple frustration: essential digital tools are often too complex, riddled with ads, or hidden behind paywalls. We believe that calculating financial goals or generating professional invoices shouldn't require a subscription or compromise your privacy.

Our Core Philosophy

Precision First

Our financial calculators account for real-world variables like inflation, not just simple math.

Privacy Centric

All data processing happens locally on your device. We don't track your private input data.

The Tool Ecosystem

Toolblaster is evolving into a high-performance digital hub. We focus on three key areas:

  • Finance: Professional SIP planning, Tax estimation, and global currency utilities.
  • Business: Professional GST billing and invoice generation for small businesses.
  • Productivity: Minimalist tools like Daily Focus and Online Notepad to clear mental clutter.
