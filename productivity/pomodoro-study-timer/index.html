🍅 What is a Pomodoro Study Timer?

The Pomodoro Study Timer is a powerful productivity tool based on the famous Pomodoro Technique developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It breaks your work or study time into highly focused, manageable chunks.

Whether you call it a traditional pomodoro timer, a study timer, or a pomofocus tool, the goal is exactly the same: to train your brain to focus completely for short bursts, knowing a rewarding break is coming soon. Instead of burning yourself out with endless hours of continuous study, this timer helps you work with time, rather than against it.

🧠 How to Use the Timer for Maximum Focus

Using our study timer is incredibly simple. Just follow these 4 steps:

  1. Pick a task: Choose your top priority for the session.
  2. Set the Pomodoro: Start the 25-minute timer and immerse yourself completely in the task. No phones, no social media.
  3. Take a Short Break: When the timer rings, take a 5-minute break. Stand up, stretch, or grab some water.
  4. Take a Long Break: After completing four 25-minute sessions (Pomodoros), reward yourself with a 15 to 30-minute long break.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why is it called 'Pomodoro'?

"Pomodoro" is the Italian word for tomato. The creator of the technique originally used a tomato-shaped kitchen timer to track his work sessions during university.

2. Can I change the 25-minute time limit?

Yes! While 25 minutes is the standard, everyone's attention span is different. You can use the settings icon below the timer to customize your study and break lengths to fit your personal workflow.

3. Does this tool work offline?

Absolutely. Once the page is loaded, the timer runs entirely in your local browser. It requires zero internet connection to count down, ensuring you can disconnect from Wi-Fi for deeper focus.

4. How is this different from a normal phone timer?

Using your phone as a timer often leads to distractions (seeing notifications when you check the time). Our web-based study timer acts as a dedicated pomofocus environment that is distraction-free and integrates directly with your daily priorities.
