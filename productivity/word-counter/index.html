What is the Smart Word Counter?

Whether you are writing an essay, preparing a speech, optimizing a blog post for SEO, or formatting a social media caption, our free online Word Counter provides instant, accurate text metrics.

We designed this tool to be completely private—your text never leaves your browser, and it auto-saves locally so you never lose your work.

Advanced Multilingual Support

Unlike basic counters that only look for spaces between English words, our Smart Multilingual Engine utilizes native NLP (Natural Language Processing) to accurately segment and count words in complex character systems.

CJK Languages

Perfect word boundaries and counting for Chinese (中文) and Japanese (日本語) without relying on spaces.

Indic & Cyrillic

Accurate counting and intelligent stop-word filtering for Hindi (हिंदी), Urdu (اردو), and Russian (Русский).

* The Keyword Density tool also includes dedicated stop-word dictionaries for English, Hindi, Urdu, Russian, Japanese, Chinese, and Tagalog (Filipino).

Key Features

  • Real-time Metrics: Instantly check your word count, character count (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs right above your editor.
  • Export Options: Instantly download your work as a .TXT text file, .DOC Word document, or .PDF right from the editor toolbar.
  • Live Grammar & Spell Check: Our engine automatically highlights duplicate words (e.g. "the the") with a red wavy underline, and spacing errors with an orange underline, acting as a lightweight free grammar checker.
  • Find & Replace: A powerful tool for writers. Search for any word, match case, and replace all instances instantly without leaving the page.
  • Writing Goals: Enable the Goal panel to set a custom word count target and track your progress in real-time with a live completion percentage bar.
  • Readability Score: Enable the Analysis panel to automatically calculate the Flesch-Kincaid grade level and see if your text is Easy, Conversational, or Advanced.
  • Keyword Highlighting & Export: Click the highlighter icon next to any top keyword to instantly locate it in your text. You can also export your keyword lists to CSV.
  • Social Media Limits: Keep an eye on the bottom right corner of the editor to ensure your text fits perfectly within Twitter's (280), Facebook's (250), Instagram's (150) and LinkedIn's (300) character guidelines.

Privacy First & Offline Support

This tool runs entirely on client-side JavaScript. Your text is never transmitted to any external server, ensuring 100% data privacy for confidential documents and scripts. The auto-save functionality utilizes your browser's local storage to prevent data loss.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Does this word counter work offline?

Yes. Once the page is loaded, the word counting and text analysis algorithms run completely offline in your browser.

2. Does it accurately count Chinese or Japanese characters?

Yes. Unlike basic counters that rely on spaces between words, our tool uses modern natural language processing (Intl.Segmenter) to accurately determine word boundaries in CJK (Chinese, Japanese, Korean) languages.

3. How does the Find & Replace work?

Click the "Find" button in the toolbar to open the panel. You can search for any word (with optional Case Matching). Matches are highlighted in blue. You can then replace them one by one or all at once.

4. How does the live grammar check work?

As you type, our engine scans for common errors like accidental duplicate words (e.g. "the the") and improper spacing before punctuation. These are highlighted with a red wavy underline, just like a traditional word processor.
