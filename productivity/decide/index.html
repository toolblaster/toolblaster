    ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

    1. What is DECIDE?

    DECIDE is a daily priority tool that helps you decide your top three priorities for today in under 60 seconds. Instead of managing long lists, DECIDE focuses on what truly matters now.

    2. Is DECIDE a daily planner?

    No. DECIDE is purely for decision clarity. It doesn't support unlimited tasks, schedules, or calendars. It is designed to be the starting point of your focus.

    3. How is it different from productivity apps?

    Most apps help you do more. DECIDE helps you choose better. By limiting you to three priorities, it reduces decision fatigue and mental clutter.

    4. Why only three priorities?

    Focus is limited. Progress on 2–3 important things per day is more effective than starting 10 things. Limits force clarity.

    5. Do I need an account?

    No. DECIDE does not require sign-up. Your data is stored locally on your device for speed and privacy.

    6. Does it work offline?

    Yes. It is a local-first tool and works without an internet connection once loaded.

    7. What happens to yesterday’s data?

    Yesterday's priorities are saved as history. We may show them as a gentle reminder but never as a judgment.

    8. Does it track streaks?

    Consistency is tracked internally to help the experience, but we don't flash high-pressure streak numbers at you.

    9. Can I use it with other apps?

    Yes. DECIDE works best as a "pre-planning" step before you open your calendars or task managers.

    10. Is it free?

    Yes. DECIDE is free to use. Premium features like cloud-sync are optional and non-intrusive.

    11. Who is it for?

    Ideal for students, professionals, and creators who are overwhelmed by lists and want clarity.

    12. Is it private?

    Yes. Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or backup.

    13. How long does it take?

    Under one minute per day. That's the design core.

    14. Keyboard shortcuts?

    Yes. Use Cmd/Ctrl + Enter to save, and Escape to close menus or cancel edits.
